Dolphin

Dolphins are very social, living in groups that hunt and even play together. Large pods of dolphins can have 1,000 members or more.

  • Scientific Name: Delphinus
  • Average Length: 2~4m
  • Average Lifespan: 20~50 years depending on the species
  • Habitat: warm, shallow waters

Dolphins are mammals and they are carnivore, so their prey is usually salmon or herrings.
